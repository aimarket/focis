This is Pomotech a fully operational timer inspired by the Pomodoro technique for efficitve work/study habits.
The case is created in fusion 360 CAD for housing. Once the design is sliced in Cura, it is ready to be printed.
Using Circut maker a custom LED design can be made and sent to pcbway.com for manufacuting.
Using an Arduino, Lithium Li-ion battery, and a 5v DC step up converter the device comes to life.
